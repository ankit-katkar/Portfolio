About Us

Hello, I'm Ankit Katkar, an experienced front-end developer and graphic designer with a strong foundation in web development and an eye for aesthetics.

As a front-end developer, I specialize in crafting beautiful and responsive websites using modern web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Angular. I have a proven track record in delivering intuitive, compelling websites that bring your online presence to life. My web design adheres to industry best-practices such as responsive web design, parallax scrolling, cross-browser compatibility and accessibility standards to ensure that your web content will be accessed seamlessly across all device types.

I am also a skilled graphic designer with a deep understanding of color theory, typography and layout design. Be it designing logos, banners or UI elements, I can leverage my design expertise to create visually stunning, engaging web pages that drive web traffic to expand your online footprint.

Above all, I prioritize time-bound and cost-effective delivery that aligns with your project objectives. I am a strong team player and have also led IT teams to design and build corporate websites and mobile apps. By hiring me, you will receive a slick, visually-appealing, intuitive website that showcases your brand to the world - all delivered on schedule and within your budget
